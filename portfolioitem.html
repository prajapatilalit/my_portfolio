The Medi-Share

Medi-share is a medical web application. it share the information of the patient in the emergency condition.

Medi-share is basically a medical website. it is data sharing platform where the patient can share their own medical history with a doctor or organization. in this platform, the doctor can edit the patient data and also views the patient medical history. and the organization can only view the patient medical data and provide the best-suited insurance policy to the patient. on the other hand, the patient can also view his own medical status and also edit basic details.

While consulting multiple specialists at the same time, there is a chance of allergic reactions by medicines prescribed to them without the knowledge of the doctors.
